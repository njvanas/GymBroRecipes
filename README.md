## Introduction

**GymBroRecipes** is created to help fitness enthusiasts find and share recipes that align with their nutritional goals. Whether you’re looking to bulk, cut, or maintain a healthy lifestyle, GymBroRecipes allows you to:

- Discover a variety of healthy, fitness-friendly recipes.
- Track calories, macronutrients, and other nutritional information.
- Save favorite meals or contribute your own recipes.
- Stay motivated with a supportive community of gym bros (and sisters)!

---

## Features

1. **Recipe Search**  
   Search for recipes by ingredients, nutritional requirements, or simply by name.

2. **Nutritional Breakdown**  
   Each recipe shows calories, protein, carbs, and fats, helping you plan your meals effectively.

3. **User Profiles & Recipe Submissions**  
   Create an account to add your own recipes, manage your contributions, and bookmark your favorites.

4. **Mobile-Responsive Design**  
   Access your meal plans and recipes on any device without compromising user experience.

5. **Customizable & Extensible**  
   Designed to be extended or adapted for personal or commercial use.
